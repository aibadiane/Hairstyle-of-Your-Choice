Tips

1) Hydration over everything! Your hair needs water so make sure you are incorporating it at the earliest stages of your haircare routine.
2) While washing your hair, make sure to focus the shampoo on your scalp. We want to give the hair follicles a healthy environment to properly grow and blossom.
3) Don't leave protective styles in for too long! This can actually cause more damage than good and lead to more breakage of the hair.
4) The porosity of your hair actually matters, so getting the right products that can perform well for your hair is important.
5) Oils can definitely promote hair growth, but too much of it can actually block the hair follicle leading to a stump in the hair growth process.
